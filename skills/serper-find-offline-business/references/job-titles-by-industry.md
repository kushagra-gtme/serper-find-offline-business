# Job Titles by Industry

When sending results to a webhook, suggest relevant decision-maker job titles based on the business category. Let the user pick which to include in the `target_job_titles` field.

## Healthcare & Medical
- **Dental**: Owner, Dentist, Office Manager, Practice Manager, Dental Office Administrator, Treatment Coordinator, Patient Coordinator, Front Desk Manager
- **Medical Practice**: Owner, Physician, Practice Administrator, Office Manager, Medical Director, Clinic Manager, Operations Manager, Patient Services Manager
- **Chiropractic**: Owner, Chiropractor, Office Manager, Practice Manager, Patient Coordinator, Front Office Manager
- **Physical Therapy**: Owner, Physical Therapist, Clinic Director, Practice Manager, Office Manager, Rehab Director
- **Veterinary**: Owner, Veterinarian, Practice Manager, Hospital Administrator, Office Manager, Lead Vet Tech

## Home Services
- **Plumbing**: Owner, General Manager, Operations Manager, Service Manager, Dispatch Manager, Estimator, Project Manager
- **Electrical**: Owner, Master Electrician, General Manager, Operations Manager, Project Manager, Estimator, Service Manager
- **HVAC**: Owner, General Manager, Service Manager, Operations Manager, Install Manager, Comfort Advisor, Sales Manager
- **Roofing**: Owner, General Manager, Project Manager, Operations Manager, Sales Manager, Estimator, Production Manager
- **Landscaping**: Owner, General Manager, Operations Manager, Account Manager, Crew Leader, Design Director, Maintenance Manager

## Professional Services
- **Law Firm**: Managing Partner, Senior Partner, Office Manager, Practice Manager, Legal Administrator, Firm Administrator, Marketing Director
- **Accounting**: Managing Partner, Owner, Office Manager, Tax Director, Engagement Manager, Client Services Manager, Practice Leader
- **Insurance**: Agency Owner, Agent, Office Manager, Account Manager, Sales Manager, Agency Manager, Operations Manager

## Food & Hospitality
- **Restaurant**: Owner, General Manager, Manager, Operations Manager, Catering Manager, Front of House Manager, Kitchen Manager
- **Bakery**: Owner, Head Baker, General Manager, Operations Manager, Production Manager, Sales Manager
- **Catering**: Owner, Catering Director, General Manager, Event Coordinator, Sales Manager, Operations Manager

## Automotive
- **Auto Repair**: Owner, Shop Manager, Service Advisor, General Manager, Operations Manager, Service Manager, Parts Manager
- **Car Dealer**: Dealer Principal, General Manager, Sales Manager, Finance Manager, Service Director, Used Car Manager, BDC Manager

## Beauty & Wellness
- **Hair Salon**: Owner, Salon Manager, Creative Director, Front Desk Manager, Operations Manager, Studio Manager
- **Spa**: Owner, Spa Director, General Manager, Operations Manager, Front Desk Manager, Wellness Director, Treatment Coordinator

## Generic (Works for Most)
If the business category doesn't match above, use these universal titles:
- Owner
- General Manager
- Manager
- Operations Manager
- Office Manager
- Director
- President
- CEO
